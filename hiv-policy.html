HIV/AIDS Policy

Introduction: Sikhula Kunye is committed to the wellbeing, dignity and rights of individuals affected by HIV/AIDS. We provide prevention, care, education and support.

Key Points

  • Non-discrimination and equality
  • Confidentiality of HIV status
  • Voluntary testing and counselling support
  • Prevention education and community awareness
  • Care and support for affected families
