HIV/AIDS Policy

Purpose: To provide clear guidance on prevention, care, confidentiality and non-discrimination for staff, volunteers and beneficiaries.

Key Principles

  • Non-discrimination and equality
  • Confidentiality of status
  • Voluntary testing and counselling
  • Prevention education and community outreach
  • Support for affected families: nutrition, school support, referrals
