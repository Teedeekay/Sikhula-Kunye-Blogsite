Welcome to Sikhula Kunye

Sikhula Kunye is a community-based non-profit organisation dedicated to Early Childhood Development (ECD) and providing HIV/AIDS support in the Ba-Phalaborwa community. We run ECD programmes, caregiver training, nutrition support and home-based care referrals.

Our Programmes

  • Early childhood learning & school readiness
  • Nutrition & food support for vulnerable children
  • HIV/AIDS counselling, referrals & home-based care
  • Caregiver workshops & community outreach
